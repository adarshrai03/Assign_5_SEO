Physics Wallah

    History Of PW:

  • Alakh Pandey, who is also popularly referred to as Physics Wallah, began his journey in 2014 when he dropped out of B Tech. fourth year and started a YouTube channel, Physics Wallah. The idea behind starting his YouTube channel was simple create a platform where he would teach simplified physics to the students.
    This realisation and his passion for teaching later inspired him to launch his YouTube channel. His YouTube channel Physics Wallah-Alakh Pndey was launched in 2014 to give students access to free online lessons. After launching his channel, he began streaming lectures on “physics” covering a wide range of topics.

    Journey Of PW:

  • In the year 2018, Alakh Pandey launched an app named Physics Wallah, which attempts to reach out to talented young students from rural backgrounds. notes, video assignments, video lectures, and exam series. A group of educators assist Physics Wallah. Additionally, there are free seminars. Additionally, for the other courses that PW provides, such as JEE NEET, UPSC, etc, the course content is readily available for download from Google Play, PW is now operating in several cities, including Patna, Pune, Lucknow, New Delhi, Varanasi, etc. For Alakh, teaching was always his first passion and through his ventures, he aims to provide affordable quality education to every deserving student .

    Web Series Physics Wallah:

  • Inspired by his success, a web series Physics Wallah was streamed on Amazon miniTV in which Shreedhar Dubey played his character.The story of Alakh Pandey, also known as Physics Wallah Sir, is truly inspiring. Beginning as a simple YouTube channel, Pandey has transformed his passion for education into a successful unicorn company. The journey of Alakh Pandey sir serves as an inspiration to all, demonstrating that with hard work and dedication, one can turn their dreams into a reality. The story of Physics Wallah is based on the real-life journey of Alakh Pandey, making it a relatable and inspiring tale for anyone looking to pursue their passions and achieve success

    Success Of PW:

  • In June 2022, Physics Wallah became India's 101st unicorn company by raising $100 million in its maiden funding round led by WestBridge Capital and GSV Ventures. During the same time, PW launched its first offline center named Vidyapeeth in Kota, Rajasthan.Over 2900 Physics Wallah IIT JEE coaching platform students recently received 98 percentile scores, making them eligible for the JEE Advanced test, the next stage in their engineering aspirations. PW also reported that over 490 kids achieved a 99.5 percentile, and over 15 pupils achieved a 99.9 percentile.
